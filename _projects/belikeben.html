Be Like Ben

2010

Be Like Ben is a website that helps you track your accomplishment of 13 virtues that Benjamin Franklin lives by.

Benjamin Franklin is one of the Founding Fathers of the United States of America. He is an author, scientist, inventor, statesman, soldier, and diplomat. In his late 20s, Ben wrote about 13 virtues for good living. He strived to live by these virtues and tracked them on paper. At the end of each day, he marked the virtues that he missed.
