MoodJam

MoodJam is an online diary that allows people to express their moods using words and patterns of color. People can also add a note to describe in more detail how they are feeling.

Go to moodjam.com >

Users' moods are visualized as stripes of color.

Users can see their moods organized by date.

Users can also see how often they use colors in a given month.

History

I created MoodJam in late 2006. Earlier that year, a bunch of HCII Ph.D. students and I discussed many ideas for mood-sharing projects. We didn't make anything, but these discussions seeded my interest in tools for sharing moods. Several months later, I visited the MIT Media Lab's web site and I really liked the colors at the top of the site. I thought what if you record moods using strips of colors.?
